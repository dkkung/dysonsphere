import Chart from '../../components/Chart.astro';

Every chart below is **live and interactive** - hover for tooltips, and use the actions menu
(top-right of each chart) to export SVG, PNG, or the Vega-Lite JSON. All are built with
dysonsphere on the standard [vega-datasets](https://github.com/vega/vega-datasets), not synthetic
data.

## Strip plot

Fuel economy by region of origin - `mark_strip()` on the `cars` dataset (jittered points with a
median tick and mean error bars).

<Chart name="strip" alt="Strip plot of miles per gallon by car origin" />

## Violin plot

Engine power by region - `mark_violin()` pairs a kernel-density silhouette with an embedded boxplot.

<Chart name="violin" alt="Violin plot of horsepower by car origin" />

## Statistical comparisons

`add_comparisons()` runs an ANOVA and draws pairwise significance brackets, with the full
descriptive + effect-size report baked into the export metadata.

<Chart name="comparisons" alt="Fuel economy by origin with ANOVA and pairwise comparison brackets" />

## Correlation

`add_correlation()` adds a Pearson coefficient, p-value, and OLS fit line - here, the classic
negative relationship between horsepower and fuel economy.

<Chart name="correlation" alt="Scatter of horsepower versus miles per gallon with an OLS fit line" />

## Categorical palette

A bar chart of mean barley yield by variety, colored with dysonsphere's perceptually uniform
category palette.

<Chart name="bar" alt="Bar chart of mean barley yield by variety" />

## Nonlinear axes

`add_log_ticks()` adds correctly spaced minor ticks to a log-scale axis - stock prices over time.

<Chart name="log" alt="Log-scale line chart of stock prices over time" />
