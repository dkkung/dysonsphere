Every dysonsphere palette, with a live preview: **click any swatch** to copy its hex codes (as a
ready-to-paste list) and restyle the charts - categorical bars and lines take evenly spaced stops (what `ds.palette(name, n)`
returns; qualitative palettes apply positionally), and the heatmap interpolates the full ramp.
See the [palettes guide](/guides/palettes/) for how to use them.
